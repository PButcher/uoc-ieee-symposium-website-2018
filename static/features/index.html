Features

What's happening at the symposium?

The event focuses on research, enterprise and knowledge transfer and will comprise:

  • Presentations (of submitted papers)
  • Research to Enterprise Pitches
  • Panel Discussion
  • Workshops
  • Awards
  • Reception Dinner

Presentations

Present your research to symposium attendees

Participants are invited to submit papers and/or posters on their research. The papers will be classified and presented through different tracks. This is not a peer reviewed conference – the idea here is to give participants the opportunity to discuss their research before an unfamiliar audience. A poster session will allow participants to interact with different researchers on their displayed research topics.

Research to Enterprise Pitches

Present your research to an expert panel

Day 1 14:20 CCU139

Interested participants are invited to pitch their research to a select panel. The precis of the pitches will be made available to sponsoring companies, participants and through the IEEE UK & Ireland Young Professionals’ social media outlets for visibility (for possible sponsorship and collaboration).

Panel Discussion

An opportunity for knowledge transfer

Day 2 09:20 CCU139

An open discussion session that allows panellists to briefly introduce/discuss their outlined focus areas and to field questions from the participants and also share their experiences and perspectives related to their journeys to their current careers. Focus will include:

  • Pathways to industry
  • Pathways to academia
  • Becoming an entrepreneur
  • Professional registration
  • Benefits of professional body membership
  • Starting MPhil/PhD (by a fresh MPhil/PhD student)
  • Finishing MPhil/PhD (by an MPhil/PhD student in their final stages)

Workshops

Intensive skills sessions

Day 1 14:20 TBC

Experts will deliver talks/training on:

  • Research Methods and Tools
  • Securing Funds and Publishing
  • Research to Enterprise
  • LaTeX

Each workshop is 30 minutes in duration. There are two streams, each running in parallel. Please be aware that workshops occur at the same time as the research to enterprise pitches.
