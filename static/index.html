IEEE YP UK&I Postgraduate STEM Research Symposium

Hosted by the Department of Computer Science, University of Chester

IEEE Young Professionals UK & Ireland and the department of Computer Science at the University of Chester cordially invite you to their inaugural postgraduate research STEM symposium. The symposium will take place at the University of Chester’s Parkgate Road campus from 28th-29th November 2018.

This symposium creates an opportunity for early career researchers, especially postgraduate research students (PhD and MPhil), to present their research and receive feedback, share ideas, network in an environment that allows them to share their challenges and learn from others, learn about relevant research tools and skills, and explore the possibilities of turning their research to enterprise. It is a 2-day event targeting STEM based postgraduate researchers and postdocs, across the country and beyond.

We are calling for submissions in the following categories:

  • Posters (A1 portrait)
  • Papers (+ Oral presentations of 15 mins)
  • Research to Enterprise Pitches

It is not necessary to make a submission to attend the symposium.

Features

What's happening at the symposium?

The event focuses on research, enterprise and knowledge transfer and will comprise of presentations, workshops, keynotes, pitches, and awards.

Presentations

Participants are invited to submit papers and posters on their research. The papers will be classified and presented through different tracks. This is not a peer reviewed conference – the idea here is to give participants the opportunity to discuss their research before an unfamiliar audience. A poster session will allow participants to interact with different researchers on their displayed research topics.

Workshops

Experts will deliver talks/training on:

  • LaTeX
  • Research Methods
  • Research Tools
  • Shut up and Write (this has been hugely successful in helping PhD students in their writing up stage)
  • Research to Enterprise (a workshop on how to turn research to enterprise)

Research to Enterprise Pitches

Interested participants are invited to pitch their research to a select panel. The precis of the pitches will be made available to sponsoring companies, participants and through the IEEE UK & Ireland Young Professionals’ social media outlets for visibility (for possible sponsorship and collaboration).

Awards

The IEEE UK & Ireland Young Professionals is sponsoring awards for:

  • Best Paper
  • Best Poster
  • Best Research to Enterprise Pitch

Reception

An optional reception with drinks and meals, providing a relaxing environment for meet-and-greet and networking. There is an additional charge (to be advised) for the reception.

Panel Discussion

An open discussion session that allows panellists to briefly introduce/discuss their outlined focus areas and to field questions from the participants and also share their experiences and perspectives related to their journeys to their current careers. Focus will include:

  • Pathways to industry
  • Pathways to academia
  • Becoming an entrepreneur
  • Professional registration
  • Benefits of professional body membership
  • Starting MPhil/PhD (by a fresh MPhil/PhD student–to talk about the challenges)
  • Finishing MPhil/PhD (by an MPhil/PhD student in their final stages – to talk about the challenges and what it feels like)

Submit

Submit your work to the symposium

To submit your work to the symposium, please provide:

  • The type of submission you wish to make (poster/paper/pitch)
  • A 200 word abstract that briefly outlines the topic of your submission. For papers, authors of accepted abstracts will be invited to submit a full paper of between 2 to 6 pages.
  • 3 keywords that best describe your topic area

The deadline for abstract submissions is 28th September 2018 at 17:00 UTC+1
